About Me

Hello! I find that it’s always a little nerve wracking to write about oneself. I’m always stuck between saying nothing, which results in a blank and boring page, and sharing too much information, such as the contents of my breakfast. I hope for your sake that I have achieved the right balance between the two.

I am excited to explore those possibilities. I am also passionate about solving problems and look forward to solving web and technical problems for companies and individuals.

I am familiar with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and everything else that is important. I hope that you will consider me for you Full Stack Web Development needs.
